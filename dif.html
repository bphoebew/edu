Differentiating between
‘b’ and ’d’

Bee is going to be a bumblebee and ‘d’ is a dog. They are facing different ways, and in order for them to be friends, students need to move them to face each other.

Shrimpala's Journey

Reading and understanding the story of Shrimpala’s journey of finding the toothpaste to brush her teeth. There will be milestones throughout the story (like Dora, Blue’s Clues) that the students will have to help Shrimpala get by helping her solve academic problems. Shrimpala’s journey will be decided by the route the students will take her on (choose your journey).
